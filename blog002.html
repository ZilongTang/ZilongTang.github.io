Design and Analysis

of 750m span Partially Earth-anchored Cable-stayed Bridge

Bachelor's degree project

Under the guidance of Prof. Zhi Fang, after consulting and reading literature, the preliminary design of 4 types of bridge is completed, and the 750m span partially earth-anchored cable-stayed bridge is calculated and analyzed.Awarded the outstanding graduation project of Hunan University.

Preliminary design: continuous rigid frame bridge, arch bridge, cable-stayed bridge, suspension bridge.

Analysis: determine the cable force, establish construction process, consider stiffness reduction, calculate internal force, deformation and vibration modes.

Design and Analysis

of 750m span Partially Earth-anchored Cable-stayed Bridge

Project innovation point

  • Innovation in structural system:

    • Partially Earth-anchored Cable-stayed Bridges are suitable for cable-stayed Bridges with relatively small side main span and large span. The design calculation of this project has roughly proved the rationality of the force of the self-balancing system in the bridge state. It gives full play to the characteristics of high compressive and tensile strength of steel box girder.

  • Innovation in construction process:

    • With reference to the gimsing system cable-stayed bridge construction scheme, the mid-span tensioned prestress is relaxed after the mid-span is closed. There are two system installation and replacement in the construction process.Through the checking calculation, the construction state structure is feasible.

  • Innovation in adjusting cable force:

    • The unknown load factor method is used to determine the cable force. First, the main tower is constrained so that it cannot shift in the model.The mid-span cable force is preliminarily calculated according to the zero-displacement method of mid-span node. Second, relax the constraint of the main tower, and determine the side span cable force according to the zero displacement method of the main tower column node. Repeat the above process to obtain the bridge cable force. Finally, the construction process is established in the model, and the tensile cable force is calculated by the formal assembly iteration method.

    Feelings

    In this project, I learned the basic steps of bridge design and mastered the relevant design calculation and analysis methods.

  • Bridge Survey:

    • The Bridges at home and abroad were investigated.In the past century, the bridge has had great development, inseparable from the efforts of the majority of researchers, designers, builders and managers.

  • Cable-stayed Bridge Calculation:

    • Cable-stayed bridge is a multi-statically indeterminate structure, and its calculation is complicated. Thanks to the development of computing power, we can solve it in a very short time.
